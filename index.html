Game Designer

In a tavern in the World of Twelve, adventurers talk about their past adventures. Very quickly, memories become tangled and everyone takes credit for it... Stop at nothing to take center stage, because, after all... it's you who deserves to become a legend!
Cesur Cebrayilov is acompetitive card laying, the aim of which is to earn more glory points than your opponents at the end of three adventures represented by cards. Each adventure begins with an adventure , which will then be completed by the souvenir cards present in the players' hands until a conclusion card . Everyone therefore contributes to a series of cards placed collectively but whose point gains are personal. It's up to you to play your cards wisely to earn more glory points than the others! For more atmosphere around the table, don't hesitate to recount all the unnecessary, and therefore essential, details of these scattered memories of adventure!
